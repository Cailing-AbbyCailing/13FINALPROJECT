How to Play

  1. Summon: Click any tile on the grid to place your cat and begin.
  2. Leap: Click glowing, highlighted tiles to move in L-shapes.
  3. Conquer: Visit every single square exactly once to win.
  4. Rewind: Use the Undo button to reverse your last leap (Max 5 times).
  5. Restart: Use Restart to start fresh on your current quest.
