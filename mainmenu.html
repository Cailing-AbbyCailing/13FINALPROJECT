How to Play

The L-Leap: You can only move in an "L-shape" (2 squares one way, 1 square perpendicular). You can leap over obstacles!

The Void: Once you step off a tile, it collapses. You can never land on the same tile twice.

The Goal: Visit every single tile on the grid exactly once to clear the board.

Game Over: If you get trapped with no legal "L-moves" left before the board is cleared, you are lost to the void.
